About Me

We Are in Part, a Sum of All We Have Met and All We Have Seen

I recently got married after returning home from my mission in Scotland and Northern Ireland. Although my heart is here, a piece of me will always me with the amazing people I met there.

I love being outside and exploring the world around me. I especially love to hike. Some of my favorites are Fifth Water Hotsprings and Turtle Wall. Check out Fifth Water Hotsprings here.

I grew up with my family in Lehi, Ut. as the oldest of 5 kids I learned to love life, work hard, and always make time for those that mean the most.
